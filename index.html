Everyone deserves to feel connected.

Make a difference today.

Right now, in low-income countries people can spend up to 70% of their monthly income on the cheapest smartphone available to them...and it is probably worse than the old unused phones you have lying around your home. We believe that everyone should have equal access to a smartphone because everyone deserves to be connected. At TechTurnaround our main goal is getting those old unused phones out of your home and into the hands of someone who needs it. We hope to destroy this 70% statistic and bring it more in line with the global average of 26%. Source: Alliance for Affordable Internet

We can change the statistics, but we need your help. TechTurnaround has a large supply of smartphones that we can provide to low income families, and other people in need. With our supply, and your charities logistics, we can start to bridge the technological inequality gap.

How it works.

We collect old smartphones. We test the usuability of the smartphones. We provide your charity with high quality smartphones You use your established charity logistics to get the device to those who need it. The recipient gets to save 70% of their monthly income and have access to a smartphone. We all celebrate!!
